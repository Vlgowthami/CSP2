About the Initiative

What is SmartPanchayat?

SmartPanchayat is a digital literacy project led by students of Vishnu Institute of Technology, with support from your local Panchayat. We aim to make rural communities confident in using digital tools like smartphones, the internet, and government apps.

Community-driven digital education
Free training and support
Practical, everyday skills

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide and empower every citizen with the knowledge and confidence to participate in the digital world safely and effectively.
